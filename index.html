HID replay - HID recorder

This program allow users to capture hidraw events and then replay them through the uhid kernel module.

In order to replay the HID events, you will need to load the module uhid which is available in kernels v3.6+.

This is mainly for debugging kernel drivers. If your goal is just to replay events, have a look at evemu: http://wiki.freedesktop.org/wiki/Evemu
